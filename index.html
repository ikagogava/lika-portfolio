About


Lika Siradze, a remarkable singer and songwriter, emerged from the underground music scene to capture the nation's heart after a standout performance on The Voice TV show. Born with an innate musical passion, Lika's journey began in the vibrant underground scene, where she cultivated her unique sound.

Her pivotal moment came when she courageously auditioned for The Voice, showcasing a voice that resonated deeply with both judges and audiences. The show served as a springboard, propelling her from the shadows of the underground to national recognition.

Lika's rise to stardom was marked by her dedication and authentic artistry. Her soulful performances and poignant songwriting struck a chord with fans, who admired her genuine expression of personal experiences.

Since The Voice, Lika Siradze has become a household name, celebrated for her incredible vocal range and chart-topping singles. Her journey symbolizes the transformative power of talent and dedication, turning an underground gem into a national sensation.
